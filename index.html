About The Journey

I joined online coding bootcamp because I wanted to take a structured and immersive approach to learning web development. Self-study is great, but I realized I needed expert guidance, a well-defined curriculum, and a community of like-minded learners to truly excel.

The bootcamp offered a practical, hands-on learning experience that mimicked real-world challenges. From building projects to collaborating on team assignments, it provided the perfect environment to bridge the gap between theory and application.


Additionally, the flexibility of an online format allowed me to balance my learning journey with my other commitments while still accessing high-quality resources and mentorship. This bootcamp has been instrumental in fast-tracking my journey to becoming a full-stack developer.
